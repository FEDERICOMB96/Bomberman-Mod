Extra Items

Extra items improve your skills during the game. In each round the counter of extra items is reset.
There are 7 extra items that can appear during the game:

Extra Bomb
You can plant one more bomb at the same time.



Fire
Increase the explosion range of your bombs by 1 square.



Maximum Fire
Maximize the blast range of your bombs (16 squares).



Skate
Increases your speed by 20.0 units/s.



Heart
You get an extra life so you can survive an explosion.



Kick
Allows you to kick bombs.



Glove
Allows you to throw bombs.
